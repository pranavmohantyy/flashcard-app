# Flashcard App

## How Spaced Repetition Works

Spaced repetition is a learning technique that incorporates increasing intervals of review. The idea is to review information just before you are likely to forget it, improving long-term retention. In this app, each flashcard has a difficulty rating and an interval that determines when it will be shown again based on your performance.

1. **Card Review**: When you review a card, you rate how well you knew the answer. This rating influences the interval before the card is shown again.
2. **Increasing Intervals**: If you answer correctly, the interval before the next review will increase. If you struggle, the interval will decrease.
3. **Tracking Stats**: The app keeps track of total cards, due cards for today, and your accuracy rate to help manage your learning progress.
